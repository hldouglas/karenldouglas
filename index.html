Welcome to Karen Douglas Consulting

At Karen Douglas Consulting we offer a wide range of child-centred parenting supports including: Custody Access Assessments, Voice of Child Reports, Mediation Services, and Parenting Consultation/Coaching. In addition, Karen is a Clinician with the Office of the Children’s Lawyer providing independent custody/access investigations and preparing reports for the court, as well as providing clinical assistance to Child’s Counsel.

Our service area covers Ottawa and surrounding areas including Kingston, Belleville, Brockville, Perth, Cornwall, Almonte, Smiths Falls, Arnprior, Carleton Place and Renfrew County.

About Me

Karen obtained a Master of Social Work Degree from Carleton University in 2003 and has over eighteen years experience working with children, youth and their families. Karen has a private practice devoted to supporting families during the challenges of separation, that is both collaborative and solution focused.

Throughout her professional career Karen has stayed current by engaging in continuing education including: Family Mediation, Clinical Interviewing of Children, Collaborative Problem Solving, Family Group Conference Facilitation and Voice of Child Reports.
